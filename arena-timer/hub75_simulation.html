This simulates the 64x32 RGB HUB75 LED matrix display driven by the RP2350B microcontroller. The 74HCT245 level shifter converts 3.3V signals from the RP2350B to 5V logic for the display.

GP0-GP5: RGB Data
GP6-GP8: Row Address
GP9: Clock
GP10: Latch
GP11: Output Enable
5V 4A: Display Power
